Transportation in Malaysia

DID YOU KNOW?

Recent surveys show that nearly all adults in Malaysia use at least one form of transport daily.

Malaysia ranks globally for high vehicle ownership (90% and above)

However only about 10% of the population utilises public transport while the others relies heavily on personal transport.

Motorcycle is the most registered vehicle in 2023 and 2024.
